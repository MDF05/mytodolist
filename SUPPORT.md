# Support

Need help with **MDF Note**? We've got you covered!

## How to Get Support

### 1. Documentation
Please check our detailed [Documentation](README.md#documentation) first. Most questions regarding installation, usage, and architecture are answered there.

### 2. GitHub Issues
If you encounter a bug or have a specific feature request, please search our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/yourusername/mdf-note/issues). If your problem isn't reported yet, feel free to [open a new issue](https://github.com/yourusername/mdf-note/issues/new).

### 3. Discussions
For general questions, ideas, or show-and-tell, join our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/yourusername/mdf-note/discussions).

## Community Guidelines

When asking for help:
-   Be polite and respectful.
-   Provide as much context as possible (screenshots, error logs).
-   Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Commercial Support

Currently, **MDF Note** is a free, open-source project and does not offer commercial support plans.
